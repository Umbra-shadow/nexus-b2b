YOU'RE IN A
DEMONSTRATION.

This is a live preview of NexusB2B. The companies you'll meet first are fictional and seeded only for demonstration — but the platform itself is fully working. You can register a real business and run genuine tests end to end.

Because the seeded companies aren't real, an AI replies on their behalf inside sessions so the flow feels complete — a demo convenience only.

How the AI works in real operation
  • —It only answers the specific question it's asked — for example a market price.
  • —It cannot read or reason about your conversation. Messages stay private between the two businesses.
  • —Anything outside that scope is declined.
Real businesses have no AI standing in. Their admins and members receive email notifications and connect with clients themselves.
